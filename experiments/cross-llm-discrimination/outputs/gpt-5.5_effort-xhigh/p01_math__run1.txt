Assuming the Boston train is headed toward New York and the New York train is headed toward Boston:

Boston train leaves at **9:00 AM** at **60 mph**.

By **10:30 AM**, it has traveled:

```text
1.5 hours * 60 mph = 90 miles
```

So the remaining distance between the trains at 10:30 AM is:

```text
215 - 90 = 125 miles
```

Now they are moving toward each other, so their closing speed is:

```text
60 + 75 = 135 mph
```

Time to meet after 10:30 AM:

```text
125 / 135 = 25/27 hours
```

Convert to minutes:

```text
25/27 * 60 = 55.56 minutes
```

So they meet about **55 minutes 33 seconds after 10:30 AM**, which is:

```text
11:25:33 AM
```

Distance from Boston:

```text
60 * (2.4259 hours) = 145.56 miles
```

Distance from New York:

```text
75 * (25/27 hours) = 69.44 miles
```
