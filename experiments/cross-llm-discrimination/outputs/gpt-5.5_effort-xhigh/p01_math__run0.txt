Work:

From **9:00 AM to 10:30 AM**, the Boston train travels alone for 1.5 hours:

```text
60 mph x 1.5 hr = 90 miles
```

So by 10:30 AM, the trains are:

```text
215 - 90 = 125 miles apart
```

After 10:30 AM, they move toward each other, so their combined speed is:

```text
60 + 75 = 135 mph
```

Time to meet after 10:30 AM:

```text
125 / 135 = 25/27 hr
```

Convert to minutes:

```text
25/27 x 60 = 55.56 minutes
```

So they meet about **55 minutes 33 seconds after 10:30 AM**:

```text
10:30 AM + 55 min 33 sec = 11:25:33 AM
```

Distance from Boston:

```text
90 + 60 x 25/27 = 145.6 miles
```

Distance from New York:

```text
215 - 145.6 = 69.4 miles
```
