Work:

First train leaves Boston at **9:00 AM** going **60 mph**.

By the time the second train leaves New York at **10:30 AM**, the first train has traveled:

```text
1.5 hours * 60 mph = 90 miles
```

So the remaining distance between them is:

```text
215 - 90 = 125 miles
```

Once both trains are moving toward each other, their combined speed is:

```text
60 + 75 = 135 mph
```

Time to meet after 10:30 AM:

```text
125 / 135 = 0.9259 hours
```

Convert to minutes:

```text
0.9259 * 60 = 55.56 minutes
```

So they meet about **55 minutes 33 seconds after 10:30 AM**:

```text
10:30 AM + 55 min 33 sec = 11:25:33 AM
```

Distance from Boston:

```text
90 + (60 * 0.9259) = 145.6 miles
```

Distance from New York:

```text
215 - 145.6 = 69.4 miles
```
