Assuming the trains are traveling toward each other:

First train head start:

```text
9:00am to 10:30am = 1.5 hours
Distance = 60 mph * 1.5 hr = 90 miles
```

So by 10:30am, the Boston train has already covered 90 miles. Remaining distance between trains:

```text
215 - 90 = 125 miles
```

Their combined closing speed:

```text
60 + 75 = 135 mph
```

Time after 10:30am to meet:

```text
125 / 135 = 25/27 hours
= 55.56 minutes
```

So they meet about **55 minutes 33 seconds after 10:30am**, which is:

```text
11:25:33am
```

Place:

```text
Boston train distance = 90 + 60 * (25/27)
= 90 + 55.56
= 145.56 miles from Boston
```

So they meet at approximately **11:25:33am**, about **145.6 miles from Boston** and **69.4 miles from New York**.
